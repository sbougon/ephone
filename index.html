// FAQ

Questions people ask.

Is RingMePlease really free? No subscriptions?

Completely free. No subscriptions, no premium tier, no in-app purchases, no ads. RingMePlease is a free fake call app with no catch — it's just a website that works like an app.

Does it work on iPhone and Android?

Yes. On iPhone, open ringmeplease.com in Safari and use Share → Add to Home Screen. On Android, open it in Chrome and use the menu → Add to Home Screen. It works as a full-screen app on both platforms.

Do I really not need to download anything from the App Store?

Correct — no app store needed at all. RingMePlease is a Progressive Web App (PWA). You add it to your home screen directly from your browser in about 5 seconds. It behaves like a native app but lives on the web.

Will people see through it? Does it look like a real call?

The incoming call screen closely mirrors iOS's native design — same layout, same buttons, same style. Add a real contact photo and name for the best effect. Anyone glancing at your screen will see what looks like a genuine incoming call.

What happens to my photo and contact name?

They're saved only on your device using your browser's local storage. Nothing is uploaded, shared, or sent to any server. There are no servers. Your data is entirely private and only exists on your phone.

Does it work if I have no internet connection?

Yes — once you've added RingMePlease to your home screen, it works completely offline thanks to a service worker that caches all the files. No signal needed when you need to escape.
